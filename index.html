    The Author

    Sir Arthur Conan Doyle

    Style: classic, clear, and richly descriptive

    Sir Arthur Conan Doyle (1859–1930) was a Scottish author, physician, and literary icon best known for creating the legendary detective Sherlock Holmes. With a masterful blend of logic, observation, and storytelling, Doyle revolutionized the mystery genre and set the standard for detective fiction. Beyond Holmes, he penned historical novels, science fiction, and spiritual works, showcasing his diverse literary talents.A visionary of his time, Doyle’s stories continue to captivate readers with intellect, intrigue, and imagination.
